About Us

Who we are

Masterscan Engineering SdnBhd,Malaysia was registered in November 2013 by a group of highly competent NDT professionals with knowledge and experience from wide range of industries, Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Power plants, Refineries, Rail road, Marine , Constructions and Heavy Engineering industries.

What we do?

MASTERSCAN Malaysia owns a variety of latest Non-Destructive Testing equipments such as Ir. 192 and Se-75 Gamma ray projectors and X-ray Machines, Ultrasonic Digital flaw detectors, Magnetic Particle testing equipments both Visible and Fluorescent type and Liquid Penetrant testing systems both visible and Fluorescent methods by solvent removable systems and Hardness testers, Positive Material Identification (PMI), Ferrite Testing etc., PWHT Equipments, PAUT & TOFD equipments

Masterscan

MASTERSCAN has highly qualified and specialized Engineering staff, NDT & Welding inspectors meeting to Client’s specific needs in the field of inspection and Engineering services and meeting to the various code and specification as required by the various industries. MASTERSCAN since its inception, has executed several prestigious projects in Singapore and overseas.

Our Services

NDT Services

We provide services in the field of NDT to cater the needs of industries ranging from small Engineering, Marine, Oil & Gas Industries.

Training & Certification

We provide training and certification for QA/QC and NDT personnel to NDT Level I and II in accordance with ASNT-SNT-TC-1A .

Marine Inspection

Our Team is professionally equipped to carry our thickness surveys on all kinds of ships such as Oil tankers, Oil Rigs, FPSO Vessels and Drill Ships.

Engineering Consultancy

The combination of practical experience and the theory rich knowledge of certified ASNT NDT professional within our training laboratory atmosphere strike the perfect hands-on balanced training experience.

Onshore & Offshore Inspections

We do Tank Inspection, Tube Inspection, Under water Inspection. We do all type of surveys either Onshore or Offshore.

Technical Manpower supply

We provide professional manpower to our Clients. All personnel in our custom designed database are pre-screened, thus providing our Clients with reliable personnel who will perform satisfactorily.
